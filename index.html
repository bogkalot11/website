About Us

We've been making healthy foods for the last 10 years

At Healthy Eats, we believe that eating well shouldn't mean sacrificing taste. Our mission is to provide you with a delightful culinary experience that not only satisfies your taste buds but also supports your well-being. From vibrant salads bursting with fresh greens and colorful vegetables to hearty grain bowls packed with wholesome goodness, our menu is thoughtfully crafted to offer you a diverse range of nutritious and delicious options. We source the finest ingredients, prioritizing local and seasonal produce whenever possible, to ensure that every dish is brimming with flavor and vitality. Whether you're a devoted health enthusiast or simply seeking a nutritious meal that doesn't compromise on taste, Healthy Eats has something for everyone. Join us on a journey to embrace a healthier lifestyle without ever compromising on the joy of eating. Indulge in our nourishing creations and discover the delicious possibilities of wholesome eating at Healthy Eats. Your taste buds and your body will thank you!

Food Menu

GUACAMOLE SALAD

Guacamole Salad is a refreshing and vibrant dish that combines the creamy texture of mashed avocados with crisp vegetables such as tomatoes, onions, and peppers. It's typically seasoned with lime juice, cilantro, salt, and pepper, giving it a zesty and flavorful taste. This salad offers a delightful mix of textures and flavors, perfect for those craving a healthy and satisfying dish with a hint of Mexican-inspired flair.

Price: ₱ 250

FRUITY CEREAL BOWL

Fruity Cereal Bowl is a delightful breakfast option featuring colorful, crunchy cereal flakes mixed with dried fruit pieces such as raisins, nuts, or banana chips. Served with cold milk, yogurt, or a dairy-free alternative, this bowl offers a satisfyingly sweet and crunchy start to the day, appealing to both children and adults alike. It's a convenient and tasty way to enjoy a nutritious morning meal packed with energy-boosting carbohydrates and a touch of fruity goodness.

Price: ₱ 200

VEGGIE WRAP

Veggie Wrap is a delicious handheld meal consisting of a soft tortilla filled with an assortment of fresh vegetables, such as lettuce, tomatoes, cucumbers, bell peppers, and carrots. Sometimes complemented with creamy spreads like hummus or avocado, along with cheese or tofu for added protein, this wrap offers a satisfying combination of flavors and textures. It's a versatile and convenient option for a quick lunch or snack, perfect for those looking for a nutritious and tasty way to enjoy their veggies on the go.

Price: ₱ 220

BLUEBERRY-CARROT MUFFIN

Blueberry-Carrot Muffin is a delightful baked treat that combines the sweet and tangy flavor of blueberries with the subtle sweetness and earthy notes of carrots. These moist and tender muffins offer a burst of fruity freshness alongside the wholesome goodness of grated carrots. Perfectly spiced with cinnamon and perhaps a hint of nutmeg, they provide a comforting aroma and taste that's both satisfying and nutritious. Enjoyed as a breakfast treat or a delightful snack, these muffins are a delightful fusion of flavors that will surely brighten your day.

Price: ₱ 280

YOGURT AND FRUIT PARFAIT

Yogurt and Fruit Parfait is a delightful layered dessert or breakfast option featuring creamy yogurt alternating with fresh fruits and crunchy granola. The yogurt provides a smooth and tangy base, while the fruits add bursts of sweetness and color. Topped with granola or nuts for added texture and crunch, this parfait offers a harmonious combination of flavors and textures. It's a refreshing and nutritious treat that can be enjoyed any time of the day, providing a satisfying balance of protein, fiber, and vitamins.

Price: ₱ 260

Cinnamon-Oatmeal Pancakes

Cinnamon-oatmeal pancakes are a delicious twist on traditional pancakes, incorporating hearty oats and warm cinnamon for a comforting and flavorful breakfast experience. These pancakes are made by blending oats into the batter, creating a hearty texture and nutty flavor. Infused with cinnamon, they offer a warm and aromatic taste that pairs perfectly with maple syrup or honey. These pancakes are not only delicious but also provide a nutritious start to your day, packed with fiber and essential nutrients.

Price: ₱ 180
